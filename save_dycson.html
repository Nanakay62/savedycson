How to Play
1
The chase. Dycson runs toward the Light on the right. Dark smoke chases him from the left — if it catches him, it's game over.
2
Answer to advance. A Bible question appears in this panel with a countdown bar. Tap an answer before time runs out.
3
3 tries per question. A wrong pick is marked and you can try again — up to 3 attempts. The correct answer is only revealed after your 3rd miss or a timeout.
4
Correct = light. Right answers push the smoke back and brighten your world. Wrong answers (or timeouts) let it surge closer.
5
Stronghold of Doubt. Every 3rd round is a timeline puzzle — tap events in the bank in the correct chronological order, then hit "Break Wall". You also get 3 attempts here.
6
Streak shields. A 3-question streak earns Armor of Light, which absorbs the penalty of your next miss. A 10-streak earns it again.
7
Eras & scoring. Every 5 questions advances you to the next biblical era, from the Patriarchs to Revelation. Your Mind Renewal Score is saved to the Scoreboard when you finish.
